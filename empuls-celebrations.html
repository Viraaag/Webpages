FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Can Empuls celebrations be set up without IT involvement?

Yes. HR admins configure all celebration programs directly from the Empuls dashboard with no IT dependency. Once your HRIS is connected (a one-time setup that IT handles), all automations — birthdays, anniversaries, onboarding — are managed entirely by HR without any further technical involvement.

What if an employee's birthday or joining date isn't in our HRIS?

Empuls also supports manual employee profile uploads via CSV, where admins or employees can enter their date of birth or joining date directly. Once the data is in the system, the automation picks it up immediately and queues the relevant celebration. You can also trigger celebrations manually for any employee at any time.

Can managers personalize the automated gift message before it sends?

Yes. Managers receive a notification before each celebration and can add a personal note, adjust the gift amount (within their budget), or preview the wishboard before it goes out. The default automation still fires on time even if the manager doesn't take any action — so no milestone is ever missed due to a busy calendar.

What reward options do employees get for their celebration?

Employees receive points credited to their Empuls wallet, which they can redeem from a catalog of 10M+ options — digital gift cards, physical merchandise, travel bookings, experiences, wellness, and charitable donations. Admins can also send non-monetary greetings and celebration cards where budget isn't applicable. All catalog options are auto-localised based on the employee's country.

How does Empuls handle festivals across different countries and cultures?

Festival campaigns are configured per event with recipient filters by country, department, or custom attributes. When a campaign sends, each employee automatically receives catalog options relevant to their region — for example, Indian employees see Diwali-relevant brands while US employees see holiday-season options. You build the campaign once; Empuls handles the regional differentiation automatically.

Can I set different reward budgets for different celebration types?

Yes. Each automation rule — birthday, work anniversary, onboarding, festival, life event — has its own reward configuration. Work anniversaries support tiered values by tenure year (e.g., ₹1,000 at year 1, ₹5,000 at year 5, ₹10,000 at year 10). Budgets are tracked in real time across all programs from the admin dashboard.
